Methodology & Data Sources

Data Collection

  • Bureau of Meteorology weather stations
  • Satellite climate observations
  • CSIRO climate projections
  • Historical climate records (100+ years)

Analysis Methods

  • Statistical trend analysis
  • Climate modeling and projections
  • Regional downscaling techniques
  • Machine learning pattern recognition

Quality Assurance

  • Automated data validation
  • Cross-validation with multiple sources
  • Peer review of methodologies
  • Regular calibration and updates
